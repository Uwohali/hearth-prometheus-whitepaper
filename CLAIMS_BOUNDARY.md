# Claims Boundary

## Permitted language

- verification-first architecture;
- designed to produce reviewable, provenance-backed evidence;
- implementation and pilot preparation in progress;
- historical canonical root identified;
- candidate release subject to review, freeze, signing and verification;
- bounded results, when linked to named evidence and limitations.

## Prohibited language without new evidence and approval

- fully verified regenerative infrastructure;
- scientifically proven platform;
- production ready;
- regulator approved;
- institutionally admitted;
- investment grade;
- guaranteed ecological, financial or health outcome;
- canonical release activated;
- independently replicated.

## Upgrade rule

A claim may be upgraded only when the corresponding evidence, reviewer, decision scope, date, limitations and revocation conditions are recorded.
